Team Phoenix

Phoenix Robotics club is one of the technical club of RV Institute of Technology and Management.Here at Phoenix Robotics Club RVITM, we know that sometimes all it takes to change the world is a little support. Since our founding in 2019, we have always been determined to make an impact on our society. The core of our efforts is to bring our team’s modern ideas and passion into function for the range of activities we’re involved in. Through all of our endeavours, we hope to display the notion behind our beliefs.

Workshops


With this initiative, our goal is to provide enormous opportunities to understand robotics. With access to the correct resources, students can discover their abilities and enhance their knowledge about robotics in the workshops. They can also learn more about our work by getting in touch with our team whenever needed.
